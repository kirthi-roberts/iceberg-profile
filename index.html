Common Questions

Everything you need to know

How is this different from a resume writer or LinkedIn optimizer? ▾
Resume writers polish what you already have. LinkedIn optimizers repackage your existing profile. Both assume your value is already visible — it just needs better words. The Iceberg Profile starts from the opposite assumption: that the most important parts of your professional value have never made it onto any page. We surface it first through a structured discovery process, then give you the language to claim it. The output is not a polished version of your old resume. It is career intelligence you did not have before you started.
Do I need to answer all 100 questions to get value? ▾
No — the Starter (30 questions) delivers genuine value and will give most professionals language they have never had before. But here is the honest truth: the tool uses your answers as training data. 30 answers lets it identify patterns. 60 answers lets it name your hidden strengths and map your hidden business value. 100 answers gives it the depth to rewrite your full career story with the precision a senior hiring manager actually responds to — plus generate a complete Resume Content Kit. More of you in means more value out.
I care about how my resume looks. Can I choose my format? ▾
Iceberg Pro includes three professionally designed templates — Executive Dark, Modern Minimal, and Technical Pro — and you can drop your content into any other format you prefer. But here is the thing worth remembering: a great resume is 90% what it says and 10% how it looks. Hiring managers at senior levels read for substance first. The Iceberg Profile takes care of the 90% that most tools ignore entirely. The chassis is yours to choose — we give you an engine worth putting in it.
I already have a strong resume. Will this still help me? ▾
Especially then. A strong resume means the surface level is covered — titles, responsibilities, maybe some metrics. What it almost certainly does not contain is your cross-domain synthesis capability, the judgment pattern you have built over a decade, the specific attributes that make you irreplaceable in a room, or the positioning language that separates you from 40 other qualified candidates. Those things are not on anyone's resume by default. They live beneath the surface. This tool excavates them.
What is your Human X-Factor and hidden business value — and why does it matter right now? ▾
Your skills and certifications are software — learnable, teachable, and increasingly replicable by AI. Your Human X-Factor is different: the deep irreducible attributes built through lived experience. Your Inner Radar — judgment when the data is incomplete. Your Vision Forge — seeing what is coming before the consensus does. Your Resilience Armor — navigating when there is no map. These took 10–20 years to build. They are your actual moat in a post-AI economy. The Iceberg Core and Pro tiers surface and name them — because you cannot claim what you cannot articulate.
Is the Iceberg Profile enough, or do I need coaching too? ▾
The Profile gives you the language. For many people that is a breakthrough on its own — a headline that stops the scroll, an executive summary that actually represents them, clarity they could not previously articulate. But language is not strategy. It does not tell you which of your options has the most leverage right now, how to navigate a promotion you have been doing the work for but not received, or what your real negotiation number is. That is the work of Dr. Roberts' 3, 4 and 6-month career programs. The professionals who engage in them typically see a material change in trajectory — not just a better document. kirthiroberts.com →
Does it matter how much detail I give in my answers? ▾

Yes — significantly. The tool reflects exactly what you give it. Thin answers produce thinner insight; rich, specific answers unlock the full depth of the report. The questions are designed to pull out depth you may not realize you have — if you find yourself writing one sentence, push for one more paragraph. The gap in output quality between a quick answer and a considered one is substantial. That said, you are never locked in: after seeing your report, you can return to any section, expand your answers, and regenerate as many times as you like. Your best report comes from your best answers.
